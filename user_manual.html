� Account Access & Login

Your journey starts by entering your secure credentials. This identifies you to the system.

SIMPLE TIP

This is like having a secret name and code to enter your favorite club! 🔑

Steps to Login:

  • 1
    Enter your Username in the first field.
  • 2
    Type your Password in the second field (it will show as dots for safety).
  • 3
    Click "Sign In" to access your personal dashboard.

❓ Trouble Logging In?

Click "Forgot Password" to receive a reset link in your email.

📊 Operational Dashboard

The dashboard provides a high-level summary of all active business metrics at a glance.

WHAT IS THIS?

Think of this as your "Control Tower" where you see all the boxes moving through the city. 📡

Understanding Metric Cards:

Total Today All items scheduled for current delivery.
In Progress Items currently being handled by heroes.
Completed Successfully delivered items today!

🗺️ Live Map Preview

The small map on your dashboard shows a "real-time" view of Nashik. You can see active delivery boys as they move toward their destinations.

� Invoice Management

This page catalogs every single delivery order (Invoice) created in the system.

KID-FRIENDLY CONCEPT

It's like a big list of "To-Do" tasks for the whole team! Each paper shows who gets what. 📝

How to manage orders:

  • 1
    Use the Filters at the top to see only "Pending" or "Delivered" orders.
  • 2
    Use the Search bar to find a specific order number.
  • 3
    Click the Eye Icon to open the full detail window.
  • 4
    Click "Export" to download a file for your computer records.

�️ Fleet Tracking

The tracking interface allows for deep monitoring of all personnel currently on the road.

This is a full-screen version of the map. It helps you see if a hero is stuck in traffic or if they are moving fast. It ensures our packages are always moving toward the finish line! 🛵💨

� Delivery Boy Portal

A specialized mobile-first view designed for our delivery personnel on the field.

FOR THE HEROES

It's like a dashboard for your bike! It tells you where to go and what to do next. 🛵

Standard Workflow:

  • 1
    Set Availability: Use the power button to signal you're ready.
  • 2
    Accept Task: Choose a task from the "Available" list.
  • 3
    Navigate: Use maps to find the hospital destination.
  • 4
    Verify: Take a photo of the signed proof and click "Submit".

Note: You can record payments (Cash or Cheque) directly within the completion form.

🔔 System Notifications

Communication is key. This portal allows administrators to broadcast updates to the team.

  • 1
    Click "Create Notification" to start a message.
  • 2
    Select your Target Audience (e.g., Everyone or just Delivery Boys).
  • 3
    Set Priority: Use "Urgent" for messages that need fast attention! 🔴
